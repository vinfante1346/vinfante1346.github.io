Executive Summary

Critical Discovery: Why Two Analyses Reached Different Conclusions

The previous analyses compared different pricing tiers:

  • Analysis 1 evaluated Kira's Growth tier (1.19% + $2/user) and recommended Kira
  • Analysis 2 evaluated Kira's Enterprise tier ($29,995/month + 14 bps) and recommended Circle

Both analyses are correct within their assumed pricing models. The optimal choice depends on your business stage, volume, and which Kira tier you qualify for.

Unified Recommendation:

Phase 1 (0-6 months): Start with Kira Growth tier if available ($2/user model). Faster implementation (7-14 days), lower costs at smaller scale, built-in offramps.

Phase 2 (6-12 months): Migrate to Circle when monthly volume exceeds $5M. Superior economics at scale, Brazil PIX support, established infrastructure.

Critical Action: Verify Kira Growth tier eligibility immediately. If only Enterprise tier ($30K/month) is available, launch with Circle from day one.

Important Note on Circle's Complete Cost

Circle offers two distinct products:

  • Circle Mint: ~1% fee to convert USD to USDC (no offramps included)
  • Circle Payments Network (CPN): Orchestration layer with offramps, requires OFI status

CPN provides complete solution without external offramp partners. Pricing above reflects CPN corridor rates. If using only Circle Mint, add 1.5-2.5% for external offramp providers (dLocal, Unlimit, etc.).

Critical Timeline Risk for Circle

OFI Status Requirement: Circle Payments Network requires Originating Financial Institution (OFI) certification. This 4-8 week vetting process cannot be expedited and may include:

  • Financial stability review
  • Compliance infrastructure assessment
  • Operational due diligence
  • Legal entity verification

BFI Partnership Complexity: Each disbursement corridor requires separate Beneficiary Financial Institution agreements, adding 6-12 weeks per region.
