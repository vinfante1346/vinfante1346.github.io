1. Customer Retention Crisis

  • 7.6% monthly decline in active customers
  • 34,671 customers lost in October (dropout data)
  • Trend shows acceleration from 29,784 in September
  • Year-to-date: 385,707 total customer losses

2. High Debit-to-Credit Ratio

  • 80% of transactions are debits (money out)
  • 1.7M debit transactions vs 430K credits
  • Ratio of approximately 4:1 sustained month-over-month
  • Suggests limited incoming value for many customers

3. Declining Credit Transactions

  • Credit transaction count down 8.9%
  • 472K → 430K credit transactions
  • Despite value growth (+10.7%), fewer incoming transactions
  • May indicate concentration among fewer customers
