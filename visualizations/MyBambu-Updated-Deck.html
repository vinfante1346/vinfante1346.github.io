Deep Dive: Circle/USDC Network for Cross-Border Payments

Circle provides enterprise infrastructure for stablecoin-based remittances with direct banking partnerships:

Circle's Advantages

  • Licensed in all 50 US states - Fully regulated and compliant
  • $40B+ USDC market cap - 2nd largest stablecoin, audited monthly
  • LatAm Banking Partnerships: Direct connections to Mexican, Brazilian, Dominican, Colombian banks
  • Circle Yield: 4-5% APY on USDC balances (potential high-yield savings product)
  • Multi-chain support: Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base (15+ chains)

How It Works

  1. Customer deposits USD in MyBambu
  2. MyBambu mints USDC via Circle API ($1 fee)
  3. USDC sent to recipient country via blockchain (minutes)
  4. Circle partner converts USDC → local currency (MXN, BRL, DOP, COP)
  5. Recipient receives money in local bank account (10-30 min total)

Total cost: $2-5 vs. $25-45 traditional wire
