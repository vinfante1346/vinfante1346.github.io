Company Overview

MyBambu is a neobank serving the US and Latin American markets, specifically targeting unbanked and underbanked Latino communities.

The platform enables secure transactions regardless of immigration status

Mission

Provide comprehensive financial services to underserved communities across the US and Latin America

Market Focus

  • Unbanked and underbanked Latino populations
  • US and Latin American markets
  • Inclusive banking regardless of immigration status
